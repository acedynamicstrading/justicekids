Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, protect and respect the personal information of all users of our platforms.

📅 Effective: 18 March 2026 🏢 Ace Dynamics Trading — Sri Lanka ⚖ Governed by Sri Lankan Law

Plain language summary: We collect only what is necessary to provide legal aid services. We never sell your data. Children's data on Justice Kids is anonymous — no names, NICs or addresses are collected. All data is stored securely. You have the right to request deletion at any time.

02

What Information We Collect

Justice LK (Case Management)

  • Client full name, NIC number, mobile number and address
  • Case facts, incident details, opposing party information
  • Documents uploaded by field officers (court orders, deeds, certificates)
  • Field officer login credentials (username and password — stored securely)
  • Case status updates and history

LexLK (Professional Platform)

  • Account registration details: name, email, WhatsApp number, nationality, NIC/passport number
  • Documents submitted for AI analysis (text content only)
  • Consultation booking details (name, contact, area of law)
  • Lawyer registration applications: Bar Association number, specialisations, qualifications

Justice Kids (Children's Platform)

  • No personal identification is collected. Children are not asked for their name, NIC, school or address.
  • Quiz completion statistics (anonymous — score and language only)
  • AI questions asked (text content, no identifying information)
  • In an emergency SOS situation: phone number (optional, child-provided) and GPS location (only if the child activates SOS and consents)

Emergency GPS Data: When a child presses the SOS button on the Minor Protection Portal, their GPS coordinates may be transmitted to the LexLK safety team to enable emergency response. This data is used solely for the purpose of locating and assisting the child. It is not stored permanently and is not used for any other purpose.

03

How We Use Your Information

  • To register, manage and track legal aid cases through Justice LK
  • To provide AI-assisted legal analysis, document drafting and Q&A services on LexLK
  • To connect clients with qualified Sri Lankan attorneys
  • To generate court petitions and case summaries for field officers
  • To send emergency notifications to the safety team when a child activates SOS
  • To improve and maintain the platforms
  • To communicate with registered users about their cases or accounts

We never use your data for advertising, profiling or marketing purposes. We do not sell, rent or trade personal information to any third party.

04

Where Data Is Stored

Our platforms currently use the following services for data storage and processing:

  • Google Sheets & Google Drive — case data, documents and lawyer applications are stored in Google's infrastructure, subject to Google's security standards
  • Cloudflare — user authentication tokens for LexLK are stored in Cloudflare's KV storage
  • Groq AI — AI analysis requests are processed by Groq's API. Submitted document text is sent to Groq for processing but is not permanently stored by Groq
  • Telegram — emergency notifications and system alerts are sent via Telegram's secure messaging infrastructure

All data is stored within secure cloud infrastructure. As the platform grows, data will be migrated to ICTA-approved government servers in Sri Lanka.

05

Children's Privacy (Justice Kids)

Justice Kids is designed specifically for children aged 10–17. We take children's privacy very seriously and have implemented the following protections:

  • No child is required to provide their name, NIC, school, address or any identifying information to use Justice Kids
  • All AI interactions are anonymous — questions are processed without any personal identifiers
  • GPS location is only activated when a child voluntarily presses the SOS emergency button
  • Emergency contact information (phone number) is optional and only used to respond to the specific emergency
  • Quiz results are logged anonymously for statistical purposes only
  • No cookies or tracking technologies are used to profile children

Parent and Guardian Notice: Justice Kids does not collect identifiable information from children. If you have concerns about your child's use of this platform or wish to understand how the emergency features work, please contact us at +94 77 362 2211.

06

Attorney-Client Confidentiality

All case information submitted through Justice LK and LexLK is treated with the same standards of confidentiality that apply to attorney-client communications under Sri Lankan law.

  • Case data is accessible only to the registered field officer who created the case and system administrators
  • Assigned attorneys receive only the case details relevant to their engagement
  • No case information is disclosed to third parties without the client's consent, except as required by Sri Lankan law
07

Data Retention

  • Case data (Justice LK): Retained for the duration of the case and for a period of 7 years thereafter, in accordance with Sri Lankan legal record-keeping requirements
  • LexLK account data: Retained while the account is active. Deleted within 30 days of a deletion request
  • Emergency GPS data (Justice Kids): Not permanently stored. Transmitted for emergency response only
  • Lawyer applications: Retained for verification purposes. Rejected applications deleted after 90 days
08

Your Rights

Under Sri Lankan data protection principles and international best practice, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion — request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements
  • Objection — object to processing of your data in specific circumstances

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at +94 77 362 2211 or +94 71 769 8609.

09

Security

We implement the following security measures to protect your data:

  • All API keys and credentials are stored as encrypted secrets — never in public code
  • LexLK user passwords are hashed using SHA-256 before storage
  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Access to case data is restricted by role — officers can only see cases in their district
  • The platform is hosted on GitHub Pages and Cloudflare, both of which maintain industry-standard security certifications
